Where interested prospects evaluate their options. This critical stage builds trust through strategic communication and keeps your brand top-of-mind during the decision-making process.

Stage Overview

Nurture Prospects Into Buyers

The consideration stage is where prospects actively compare options. Through personalized email sequences and strategic retargeting, we stay present in their decision-making process while building the trust needed to close.

At this stage, we collect sales information that helps assess lead quality—qualification criteria, objections, and lost reasons. This data feeds back into marketing to improve campaign targeting.

Personalization

Dynamic content based on behavior, industry, and funnel stage. Each email feels personally written for the recipient.

Behavioral Triggers

Send targeted emails based on actions—page visits, downloads, email opens, link clicks—not just time delays.

A/B Testing

Continuous testing of subject lines, content, CTAs, and send times to maximize engagement and conversion.
