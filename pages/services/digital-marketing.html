CRM-Driven Marketing

Marketing That Talks to Your CRM

Every campaign we build includes custom CRM integration. We push leads directly into your system and pull sales feedback back into your marketing channels—creating a closed-loop system that continuously improves.

CRM Properties Setup

We configure your CRM with the right properties to capture valuable lead information beyond just name and email—qualification data that helps your sales team close faster.

Bi-Directional Sync

Leads flow into your CRM automatically, and sales outcomes flow back to optimize campaigns. This feedback loop trains algorithms to find more high-quality prospects.

Attribution Tracking

Know exactly which campaigns, keywords, and ads are driving revenue—not just clicks. Make data-driven decisions that maximize your marketing investment.

Marketing Automation

Automated email sequences, lead scoring, and nurture campaigns that work 24/7 to move prospects through your funnel without manual intervention.

Lead Qualification

Intelligent lead scoring based on behavior, demographics, and engagement. Your sales team focuses only on the prospects most likely to convert.

Continuous Optimization

We don't set and forget. Weekly optimization cycles ensure your campaigns improve over time, delivering better results month after month.
